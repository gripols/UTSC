# Order of Operations (When There Are No Brackets)

1. NOT $(\neg)$ **DO IT FIRST**
2. AND ($\wedge$)
3. OR ($\lor$)
4. $\forall$ / $\exists$ 
5. $\rightarrow$ / $\leftrightarrow$ **DO IT LAST**
