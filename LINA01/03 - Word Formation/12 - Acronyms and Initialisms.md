## Acronyms and Initialisms

### Acronyms

- Formed from **initial letters** and **pronounced as words**.
  - E.g. *NASA*, *RADAR*

### Initialisms (Abbreviations)

- Formed from **initial letters**, pronounced **letter-by-letter**.
  - E.g. *FBI*, *CD*
