## Suppletion

- A case where a word changes **entirely**, rather than through affixation or internal change.
- No phonological relationship between the forms.

### Examples

- *Go* → *Went*  
- *Be* → *Was*, *Were*

> Suppletion often fills irregular gaps in inflectional paradigms.
